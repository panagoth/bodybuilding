Workout Plan

Week 48 (27 nov - 3 dec)

Monday: Chest and Triceps

  • 1. Bench Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps (80 kg)
  • 2. Incline Dumbbell Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • 3. Close Grip Flat Bench Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • 4. Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • 5. Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Wednesday: Back and Biceps

  • 1. Pull-Ups: 4 sets to failure (or as many as you can do)
  • 2. Bent-Over Row: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • 3. Dumbbell Curl: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • 4. Hanging Leg Raise: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Friday: Legs and Lower Back

  • 1. Squats: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • 2. Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • 3. Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • 4. Hanging Leg Raise: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
