Workout Plan

Week 47 (20 - 26 nov)

Monday: Upper Body Focus

  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps (80 kg)
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Pull-Ups: 3 sets to failure
  • Bent-Over Row: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Curl: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Close Grip Flat Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Wednesday: Lower Body & Core

  • Squats: 4 sets of 8-10 reps(90 kg, 8 reps)
  • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6-8 reps(100 kg, 8 reps)
  • Hanging Leg Raise: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (Optional)

Friday: Full Body & Balance

  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 6-8 reps(try 120kg, 6 reps)
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Pull-Ups: 3 sets to failure
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Bent-Over Row: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Curl: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Close Grip Flat Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
